FAQ

Know what to expect before you subscribe.

Does Tab Relief guarantee exact memory savings?

No. Chrome controls the final memory behavior. Tab Relief helps by sleeping safe background tabs and reducing tab clutter, but exact savings vary by site and device.

Does Tab Relief store my card details?

No. Payments are processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. Tab Relief does not store card numbers on its own servers.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Subscriptions can be managed through the billing portal provided by ExtensionPay and Stripe. You can also contact support for help.

What data does the extension use?

Tab Relief reads tab titles, domains, URLs, and tab states so it can decide what can be slept, protected, saved, or closed. See the Privacy Policy for details.
