What it actually does

Four tools for a browser that gets messy every day.

Tab Relief is built around a simple job: keep Chrome under control without making you manually inspect every tab every day.

01

Sleep inactive tabs

Use Chrome's native tab discard behavior to rest background tabs and bring them back when you open them again.

02

Auto Sleep rules

Let Tab Relief quietly sleep inactive tabs after a chosen interval, with a sensible 15-minute default.

03

Workspace restore

Save a whole messy window as a named workspace, clean up, and restore the saved set later when the task comes back.

04

Close matching tabs

Pick common presets or type your own words to find matching tabs, then close them in a controlled review step.

FAQ

Know what to expect before you subscribe.

Does Tab Relief guarantee exact memory savings?

No. Chrome controls the final memory behavior. Tab Relief helps by sleeping inactive tabs and reducing tab clutter, but exact savings vary by site and device.

Does Tab Relief store card details?

No. Tab Relief does not store card numbers.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Open Billing in the extension to manage or cancel. Paid plans renew until canceled.

What data does the extension use?

Tab Relief reads tab titles, domains, URLs, and tab states so it can decide what can be slept, protected, saved, or closed.
