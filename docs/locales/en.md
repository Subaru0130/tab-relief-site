# Locale: English

## Native Quality Status

Status: production-supported

English is the source locale for the landing page, but it still needs a native product-copy pass before release.

## Tone

- Calm, practical, and trustworthy.
- Prefer a clear product promise over clever slogans.
- Keep pricing, trial, and cancellation wording concrete.
- Avoid internal design labels or abstract SaaS jargon.

## Billing Vocabulary

- Use "14-day free trial" for the card-free trial.
- Use "does not require a card" for the trial setup.
- Use "will not become a paid plan automatically" when explaining what happens after the trial.
- Use "billing page" for subscription, receipts, cards, and cancellation.

## Forbidden Literal Phrases

- "Payment method required"
- "No automatic charge"
- "account page"
- "billing portal"
- "Start without a card"
- "Memory estimate"

## Marketing And Hero Copy

- The hero should make the product job clear in five seconds: keep Chrome lighter without forcing the user to close important tabs.
- Product previews should support the promise, not look like fake buttons.
- Metrics must show a representative useful state, never "0 MB" as the main proof.
